Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 • Yellow Iron Yard LLC

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. This policy explains how Yellow Iron Yard LLC ("we," "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on YellowIronYard.com.

How We Use Cookies

Category Purpose Examples
Essential Site functionality, security, form submissions Session cookies, CSRF tokens, Cloudflare
Analytics Understand how visitors use our site Google Analytics (_ga, _gid)
Advertising Measure ad effectiveness, serve relevant ads Google Ads, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight
Functional Remember preferences on return visits Language settings, form pre-fill

Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregated data about site usage. Google Analytics cookies expire between 24 hours and 2 years.

We also use advertising pixels from Google Ads and Meta (Facebook) to measure campaign performance and serve relevant ads. These tools may track browsing behavior across websites. Advertising partners handle collected data under their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Blocking cookies may affect site functionality. You can also opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Manage advertising preferences at optout.aboutads.info, optout.networkadvertising.org, adssettings.google.com, or facebook.com/adpreferences.

Our site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, but the controls above let you manage tracking directly.
