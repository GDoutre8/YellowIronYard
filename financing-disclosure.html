Financing Disclosure & Communication Consent

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 • Yellow Iron Yard LLC

Financing Marketplace Disclosure

Yellow Iron Yard LLC is a commercial equipment financing marketplace and is not a direct lender in all transactions.

We connect your business with lending partners — including banks, credit unions, and commercial finance companies — based on your equipment needs, credit profile, and qualifications.

Rates, Terms & Approval

  • Credit Approval Required: All financing is subject to lender approval. Approval is not guaranteed.
  • Rates & Terms Vary: Interest rates, fees, repayment periods, and down payment requirements depend on the lender, your creditworthiness, equipment type, and deal structure.
  • No Guarantee of Funding: Submitting an application is not an offer to lend or a binding agreement.
  • Subject to Change: Rates or terms shown on our website are illustrative only and may change without notice.

Underwriting & Information Sharing

By submitting an application, you agree that:

  • Your business and personal financial information will be shared with lending partners for underwriting
  • Lenders may run credit inquiries (soft pull for pre-qualification, hard pull for formal underwriting)
  • Once submitted to a lender, their privacy policy and terms govern your data
  • Yellow Iron Yard may receive compensation from lenders for funded transactions
