Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 • Yellow Iron Yard LLC

Introduction

Yellow Iron Yard LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a commercial equipment financing marketplace connecting businesses with lending partners. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

Information We Collect

When you inquire about financing or submit an application, we may collect:

  • Name, title, phone, and email
  • Business name, address, entity type, industry, and years in business
  • Equipment details, requested financing amount, and term
  • Revenue, EIN, Social Security Number (for guarantor evaluation), bank statements, and trade references
  • Credit history (obtained with your consent via soft or hard inquiry)

Our website also automatically collects technical data including IP address, browser and device type, pages visited, and cookie/pixel identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to match you with lending partners, submit applications on your behalf, communicate about your inquiry or application status, improve our services, prevent fraud, and send marketing communications (with your consent).

Sharing Your Information

Lending Partners: We share application data with lending partners for underwriting. Once shared, the lending partner's privacy policy governs their use of your data.

Service Providers: We work with providers for hosting, analytics, CRM, and email delivery. They are contractually required to protect your information.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Business Transfers: Your information may transfer in a merger or acquisition. We will notify you of any ownership change.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Communications

By providing your phone number or email, you may receive messages about your financing inquiry, including application updates and approval notifications. Marketing messages require your consent.

Opt out anytime: reply STOP to any SMS, click "unsubscribe" in any email, or contact us directly. Opting out of marketing does not affect transactional messages for active applications. Message and data rates may apply.

Cookies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for analytics and advertising. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Data Security & Retention

We protect your information with encryption (SSL/TLS), access controls, and secure storage. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your data. We retain information as long as needed for business purposes, legal obligations, and commercial lending record-keeping requirements.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations). You may also opt out of marketing communications or disable cookies via browser settings. Contact us to exercise these rights.

California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined under California law. Contact us to submit a request.
